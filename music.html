My Music

I've been making music for much longer than I've been making art or websites. Music was one of my first passions, and many of my Christmas and birthday gifts over the years have been musical instruments or software. The past few years, I've really focused on using FL Studio, a music production software, to make lofi music.

What is Lofi?

Lofi music, short for "low fidelity," is a genre that captivates listeners with its distinctive charm and mellow vibes. Rooted in simplicity and characterized by its intentionally unpolished quality, lofi music creates a tranquil soundscape that serves as a sonic sanctuary for many.

At its core, lofi embraces imperfections, often featuring soft background noise, vinyl crackles, and deliberate lo-fi recording techniques. This intentional rawness gives the music an authentic and nostalgic feel, as if it's echoing from a cozy, well-loved record player.
