My Websites

Over the past few years, I have been honing my skills of website design and creation. Not only have I completed several courses about web development, but I have also taken on several personal projects.

On the left, you'll see a few of my favorite websites that I have designed and developed over the past year. You can visit these websites yourself by clicking the button under each description, but I have also included a brief screen recording that walks through the websites for your convienence.

As you can see, in the past I have favored the single column layout. However, after this semester and learning more about web design principles, I have started to favor a 2 column design. The 2 column design makes it easier to folow the F-design pattern, and I create mostly text-based sites, so it makes more sense for me to use the 2 columns for most applications.
